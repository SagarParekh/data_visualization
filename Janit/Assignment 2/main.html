Performance of countries at the Australian Open


Have you wondered how countries as a whole perform at the Australian Open?

I've always wondered, so I decided to visualise it. Let's have a look. The map shows a choropleth of the number of matches played by each country. Spainish players have played the most number of matches, amounting to 338, followed by France at 322.

It is interesting to note that despite their size, European countries have produced the most number of players that have played at the Australian Open. Tennis seems to be a popular sport there. Populous countries like India and China have played only 4 and 3 matches respectively. The ATP should consider marketing tennis a bit more to these countries.

On selecting one country from the map and another from the drop down list, we can compare the average performace of these countries every year on these statistics - Aces, First Serve In %, Double Faults, % Second Points Won and Break Points Won
